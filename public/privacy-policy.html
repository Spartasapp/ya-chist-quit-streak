Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-04-27

BadHabits is an abstinence tracking app for alcohol and cigarettes. This policy explains what data is processed and how it is used.

Data We Store

  • Daily checkmarks and streak records
  • Relapse records (date/time and optional note)
  • UI preferences (language and theme)

This data is stored locally in your browser using IndexedDB and localStorage. We do not require account registration.

Advertising

The app may display ads via Google AdSense. AdSense may process technical data (such as device/browser identifiers, IP, and interaction signals) according to Google policies.

For details, refer to Google Privacy Policy and AdSense Program Policies .

How To Delete Data

You can clear local app data at any time by deleting site data in browser settings (IndexedDB/localStorage), or by uninstalling app storage on your device.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: support@your-domain.com

Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. Updated versions will be published on this page.
